Who Are We?

Wild Portraits Safaris is a safari outfitter based in Kenya. We are dedicated to provide wildlife enthusiasts, campers, hikers, as well as photographers with the best game viewing experience possible.

Certified Professions
Wild Portrait Safaris is a professional safari outfitter. We help you plan your itineraries packages competently to give you the most authentic safari as per your aspirations. We attribute our success to a flexible team of professionals. Wild Portraits Safari guides are all accredited members of KPSGA (Kenya Professional Safari Guides Association) at silver and gold levels. These are highly qualified guides who will provide you with best experience in the field.

Why Travel With Us
Our guides are highly qualified and experienced in working with international photographing and film teams. Therefore, they are an essential factor in the success of your safari.
Wild Portrait Safaris has extensive experience in guiding and creating itinery packages in East Africa for a variety of clients. As a travel partner we bring vast experience to compliment your desired safari.
Every safari is unique and requires different types of vehicles. We provide customized safari vehicles to compliment your photographic, family or budget safari.
We handpick hotels and accomodation that specificaally suits your safari package. This is an important aspect of the safari, since it serves as a perfect foil and unwinding location after a successful day in the wild.
Another reason to travel with us, as opposed to planning and taking your own trip, is cost. We believe our trips represent good value, and once you've considered all of the expenses involved in running all-inclusive trips, we think you will too.
